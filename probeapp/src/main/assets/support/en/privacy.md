# Privacy Policy (Offline)

This page explains what information the app may process and why. You must review it on first launch and choose **Agree** or **Decline**.  
**Note: This is a template and must be reviewed by legal before release.**

## Information we process

- Calculation inputs you enter (stored locally to restore last inputs)
- Crash logs (stored locally only; you can copy and share with support)

## How we use it

- Only for on-device calculations and usability improvements
- No upload to servers without your explicit consent

## Storage & retention

- Stored on your device by default
- You can clear local data from system settings or the app's reset actions

## Your rights (GDPR/CCPA summary)

- Access and correction
- Deletion (clear local data)
- Withdraw consent (the app may stop working after withdrawal)
